# Dashboard

The Erlin AI dashboard is your centralized hub for managing all aspects of your content strategy and brand analytics.

## Key Features

### Interactive Widgets

Access real-time data through customizable widgets:

- **Traffic Analytics** - Website traffic and engagement metrics
- **Media Sources** - Content performance across channels
- **Opportunities** - Content gaps and recommendations
- **Recent Activity** - Latest updates and changes
- **Performance Trends** - Historical data visualization

### Quick Actions

Perform common tasks directly from the dashboard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Content">
    Start a new article with one click
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Analytics">
    Check brand mentions and performance
  </Step>

  <Step title="Manage Brand">
    Update company profile and settings
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Library">
    Browse your content collection
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Customization

Personalize your dashboard experience:

- **Widget Arrangement** - Drag and drop to reorganize
- **Custom Views** - Save different layouts for different needs
- **Filter Options** - Focus on specific metrics or time periods
- **Theme Settings** - Light or dark mode
