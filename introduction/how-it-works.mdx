# How Erlin AI Works

Learn about the technology and workflows that power Erlin AI.

## Content Generation Workflow

Erlin AI uses a sophisticated multi-step process to generate high-quality content:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Information Gathering">
    You provide the topic, keywords, target audience, and any specific requirements for your content.
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI Research & Analysis">
    Our AI analyzes your requirements and:
    - Researches the topic comprehensively
    - Identifies key points and structure
    - Generates a research brief with outline
  </Step>

  <Step title="Content Generation">
    Using the research brief, our AI:
    - Creates well-structured content
    - Applies your brand voice and tone
    - Optimizes for SEO and readability
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review & Refinement">
    You review and edit the content using our advanced editor:
    - TipTap rich text editor
    - Real-time collaboration
    - Version control
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Brand Voice Analysis

Erlin AI maintains your brand consistency through:

1. **Voice Detection** - AI analyzes your existing content to understand your brand voice
2. **Tone Mapping** - Creates a profile of your communication style
3. **Content Alignment** - Ensures all generated content matches your brand
4. **Continuous Learning** - Improves understanding as you create more content
