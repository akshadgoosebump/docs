# Quick Start Guide

Get started with Erlin AI in just a few simple steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up">
    Create your Erlin AI account at [erlin.ai](https://erlin.ai)

    - Choose your plan
    - Verify your email
    - Complete your profile
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Your Brand Profile">
    Configure your company information and brand voice

    - Add company details
    - Define your target audience (ICP)
    - Set up brand tone analysis
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Your First Article">
    Create your first piece of content with AI assistance

    - Select article type
    - Provide topic details
    - Review and edit generated content
  </Step>

  <Step title="Explore the Platform">
    Discover all Erlin AI features

    - Check your analytics dashboard
    - Set up integrations
    - Explore the content library
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Your First Article

Here's how to create your first article:

1. **Navigate to Content Generation** - Click "New Article" from the dashboard
2. **Choose Article Type** - Select from 7 available article types
3. **Fill in Details** - Provide topic, keywords, and any specific requirements
4. **Generate Research Brief** - Review AI-generated outline and research
5. **Create Content** - Generate the final article with our AI
6. **Edit & Refine** - Use the rich text editor to polish your content
7. **Save or Export** - Save to your content library or export in your preferred format

<Note>
  Your first article is free! No credit card required for trial accounts.
</Note>
