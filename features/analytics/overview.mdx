## Key Features

### Real-time Brand Monitoring

Stay informed about your brand's online presence:

- **Instant Alerts** - Get notified of important mentions
- **Sentiment Analysis** - Understand public perception
- **Source Tracking** - Know where mentions originate
- **Historical Data** - Track changes over time

### Competitor Analysis

Understand your competitive landscape:

- **Performance Benchmarking** - Compare against competitors
- **Content Tracking** - Monitor competitor content strategy
- **Keyword Analysis** - Identify competitor keywords
- **Market Insights** - Understand market positioning

### Custom Analytics

Create analyses tailored to your needs:

- **Custom Collections** - Group related data points
- **Flexible Queries** - Ask specific questions with AI
- **Automated Reports** - Schedule regular analysis
- **Data Export** - Export for further analysis
