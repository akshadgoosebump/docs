# Content Generation

Create engaging, high-quality content across multiple formats with AI-powered assistance.

## Content Generation Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Article Type">
    Select the type of content you want to create from 7 available options.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide Information">
    Enter topic details, keywords, target audience, and specific requirements.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Research Brief">
    AI creates a comprehensive outline and research brief for your review.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Content">
    Generate the full article based on the approved research brief.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Edit & Refine">
    Use our rich text editor to polish and customize your content.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save or Export">
    Save to your content library or export in your preferred format.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Features

- **Brand Voice Integration** - Content matches your brand tone automatically
- **SEO Optimization** - Built-in SEO best practices and keyword integration
- **Rich Text Editor** - Advanced TipTap editor for customization
- **Research Briefs** - AI-generated outlines before full content creation
- **Version Control** - Track changes and revert when needed
- **Multi-Format Export** - Export to various formats
