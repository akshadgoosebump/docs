# Article Types

Erlin AI supports 7 distinct article types, each optimized for specific content needs.

## Available Types

### Product Reviews

Generate comprehensive product reviews with detailed analysis, pros and cons, and recommendations.

**Best for:**
- E-commerce content
- Affiliate marketing
- Tech reviews
- Product comparisons

**Features:**
- Structured format
- Feature analysis
- Pros and cons sections
- Rating recommendations

---

### Product Comparisons

Create side-by-side product comparisons highlighting differences and similarities.

**Best for:**
- Buyer's guides
- Product selection content
- Feature comparisons
- Decision support

**Features:**
- Comparison tables
- Feature highlights
- Use case scenarios
- Recommendations

---

### How-to Guides

Step-by-step instructional content for various topics and use cases.

**Best for:**
- Tutorial content
- Educational articles
- Process documentation
- Training materials

**Features:**
- Numbered steps
- Clear instructions
- Tips and warnings
- Visual descriptions

---

### How-to Product Guides

Product-specific tutorials and implementation guides.

**Best for:**
- Product documentation
- User guides
- Setup instructions
- Feature tutorials

**Features:**
- Product-focused steps
- Setup guidance
- Troubleshooting tips
- Best practices

---

### Blog Posts

SEO-optimized blog articles aligned with your brand voice.

**Best for:**
- Company blogs
- Thought leadership
- Industry insights
- Content marketing

**Features:**
- SEO optimization
- Brand voice integration
- Flexible structure
- Engaging content

---

### Press Releases

Professional press release format for announcements and updates.

**Best for:**
- Product launches
- Company announcements
- Partnership news
- Event coverage

**Features:**
- Standard PR format
- Professional tone
- Key information structure
- Contact details

---

### Guest Posts

Guest blogging content tailored for external publications.

**Best for:**
- Guest posting campaigns
- External publications
- Industry contributions
- Link building

**Features:**
- Publication-specific tone
- Author bio integration
- Topic expertise
- Editorial standards

---

## Choosing the Right Type

<Note>
  Not sure which article type to choose? Consider your goal:
  - **Inform** → How-to Guides or Blog Posts
  - **Compare** → Product Comparisons
  - **Review** → Product Reviews
  - **Announce** → Press Releases
  - **Contribute** → Guest Posts
</Note>
