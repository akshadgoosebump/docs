# Content Generation Workflow

Erlin AI uses a sophisticated multi-step workflow to ensure high-quality, brand-aligned content.

## The 3-Step Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Information Gathering">
    **What happens:**
    - You select your article type
    - Provide topic and keywords
    - Define target audience
    - Add specific requirements

    **Duration:** 2-3 minutes

    **Output:** Detailed content brief ready for AI processing
  </Step>

  <Step title="Research Brief Generation">
    **What happens:**
    - AI researches your topic comprehensively
    - Analyzes competitor content
    - Creates detailed outline
    - Identifies key points and structure
    - Suggests headings and flow

    **Duration:** 1-2 minutes

    **Output:** Comprehensive research brief and content outline
  </Step>

  <Step title="Content Generation & Editing">
    **What happens:**
    - AI generates full article based on research brief
    - Applies your brand voice
    - Optimizes for SEO
    - Creates structured, engaging content
    - You review and edit with rich text editor

    **Duration:** 2-3 minutes + editing time

    **Output:** Polished, ready-to-publish content
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Workflow Benefits

### Quality Assurance

By breaking content creation into steps, you maintain control over:

- **Topic Direction** - Approve the outline before full generation
- **Content Structure** - Review and adjust the research brief
- **Brand Alignment** - Ensure tone matches your voice
- **SEO Optimization** - Verify keyword integration

### Efficiency

The workflow is designed for speed without sacrificing quality:

- **Total Time:** 5-10 minutes for complete article
- **AI Processing:** 3-5 minutes automated
- **Human Review:** 2-5 minutes editing
- **Batch Creation:** Generate multiple articles simultaneously

## Step-by-Step Example

### Step 1: Information Gathering

```
Article Type: Blog Post
Topic: "Benefits of AI in Content Marketing"
Keywords: AI content, marketing automation, content strategy
Target Audience: Marketing professionals
Word Count: 1500-2000 words
Tone: Professional, informative
```

### Step 2: Research Brief

AI generates:
- Introduction approach
- 5-7 main sections
- Key points for each section
- Supporting statistics and data
- Conclusion strategy
- SEO recommendations

### Step 3: Content Creation

AI produces:
- Full article with all sections
- Engaging introduction
- Well-structured body content
- Data-backed insights
- Compelling conclusion
- Meta description and title

## Best Practices

  <Accordion title="Provide Detailed Information">

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Review the Research Brief">
    Always review and approve the research brief before proceeding. This ensures the content direction aligns with your expectations.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use the Editor Effectively">

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Save Templates">
